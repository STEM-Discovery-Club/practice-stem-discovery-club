Our Programs

Hands-On. Minds-On.
Future-Ready!

Every program is designed to be engaging, accessible, and exciting — no prior experience needed. Just curiosity and enthusiasm!

Chemistry

Mix, react, and discover! Students will explore chemical reactions, safe lab techniques, and the science behind everyday materials through hands-on experiments. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Science Free

Physics

Force, energy, light, and sound - students will investigate the laws of the universe through demonstrations, simple machines, and real-world challenges. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Math and Engineering Free

Biology

Explore life! From cells to ecosystems, students will investigate living things through specimen observation, and nature-based field activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Science & Lab Free

Astronomy

Reach for the stars! Students will explore planets, constellations, black holes, and space exploration through stargazing sessions, models, and NASA-inspired activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

All Ages Stargazing Science and Technology

Plant Science

Grow, observe, and discover! Students will learn about photosynthesis, plant life cycles, and sustainability by planting seeds, conducting growth experiments, and building mini gardens. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Science & Learn Free

Health Science

Understand the amazing human body! Students explore anatomy, nutrition, and wellness through models, and hands-on activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Body & Wellness Science

Engineering

Design, build, test, and solve real-world problems through creative teamwork and innovative thinking. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

All Ages Team-Based Math and Engineering

Math

Apply math in exciting and practical ways through games, puzzles, and real-world activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Math & Puzzles Free
