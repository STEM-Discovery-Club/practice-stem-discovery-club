Our Programs

Hands-On. Minds-On.
Future-Ready!

Every program is designed to be engaging, accessible, and exciting — no prior experience needed. Just curiosity and enthusiasm!

Chemistry Program Photo

Chemistry Lab

Mix, react, and discover! Students explore chemical reactions, safe lab techniques, and the science behind everyday materials through colorful, hands-on experiments. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Experiments Free
Physics Program Photo

Physics in Motion

Forces, energy, light, and sound — students investigate the laws of the universe through exciting demos, simple machines, and real-world challenges. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Demos Free
Biology Program Photo

Biology Explorers

Zoom in on life! From cells to ecosystems, students investigate living things through microscopes, specimen observation, and nature-based field activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Nature & Lab Free
Astronomy Program Photo

Astronomy & Space

Reach for the stars! Students explore planets, constellations, black holes, and space exploration through stargazing sessions, models, and NASA-inspired activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

All Ages Stargazing Free
Plant Science Program Photo

Plant Science

Grow, observe, and discover! Students learn about photosynthesis, plant life cycles, and sustainability by planting seeds, conducting growth experiments, and building mini gardens. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Grow & Learn Free
Health Science Program Photo

Health Science

Understand the amazing human body! Students explore anatomy, nutrition, and wellness through interactive models, hands-on activities, and visits from health professionals. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Body & Wellness Free
Engineering Program Photo

Engineering Challenges

Design, build, test, and solve real-world problems through creative teamwork and innovative thinking. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

All Ages Team-Based Free
Math Program Photo

Math in Action

Apply math in exciting and practical ways through games, puzzles, and real-world activities. Activities are leveled so every age group is appropriately challenged.

Ages 6–12 Games & Puzzles Free
