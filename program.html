Our Programs

Hands-On. Minds-On.
Future-Ready!

Every program is designed to be engaging, accessible, and exciting — no prior experience needed. Just curiosity and enthusiasm!

Biology

Explore living things up close — from cells to ecosystems — through hands-on experiments and observation.

Ages 6–12 Hands-on Free

Chemistry

Mix, react, and discover through safe, guided experiments that reveal the science behind everyday materials.

Ages 8–14 Hands-on Free

Physics

Investigate motion, energy, and forces through playful experiments that make big ideas easy to grasp.

Ages 8–14 Hands-on Free

Astronomy

Look up and wonder — students explore planets, stars, and space through models and stargazing activities.

All Ages Stargazing Free

Grow, observe, and learn how plants live and thrive through gardening projects and simple botany experiments.

Ages 6–12 Hands-on Free

Discover how the human body works and build healthy habits through age-appropriate, hands-on activities.

Ages 8–14 Hands-on Free

All Ages Team-Based Free

Math

Apply math in exciting and practical ways through games, puzzles, and real-world activities.
