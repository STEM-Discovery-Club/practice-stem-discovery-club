Why Your Support Matters

We are a 100% student-run club with zero paid staff. Every single dollar donated goes directly toward student programs, materials, and events — nothing else.

San Antonio students deserve access to quality STEM education regardless of their background. Your donation helps make that a reality.

  • All programs are completely free for participants
  • Donations fund supplies, event costs, and materials
  • 100% of funds go directly to programs
  • We are a youth-led nonprofit focused on community impact
