Our Core Values

Youth-Led Impact

High school students leading the next generation into STEM careers and innovation. We believe young people have the power to teach and inspire each other.

Community Driven

Rooted in San Antonio, serving our local schools and neighborhoods. Every session we run stays close to home and close to the community we love.

Every session sparks curiosity that lasts a lifetime. We measure success not by test scores but by the excitement on a student's face when something clicks.

How We Got Started

STEM Discovery Club was founded by a group of students who wanted to give back to their community by sharing their love of science and technology with younger kids.

What started as a small after-school project quickly grew into a full program serving multiple schools across San Antonio. Today, we're proud to be making a real difference — one hands-on session at a time.

We are 100% student-run, volunteer-powered, and community-supported. Every event we plan, every activity we run, and every smile we see is what keeps us going.
