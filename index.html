Our Mission

Building the Future,
Together!

We are a group of high schoolers who want to make an impact in our community by helping young students who are interested in the STEM field to explore the different branches of science and expand their knowledge through hands-on, learning experiences.

Why?

Have you ever noticed that kids that are "geniuses" are usually children who were exposed to these programs early on? Nationwide about 6-7% of students are considered "gifted," not because they were born with it, but because they were surrounded by things that helped them develop a more advanced way of thinking. We can increase this percentage of "gifted" kids together by hosting small, fun activities that allow us to help kids become more exposed to a competitive but amazing field.

However, we can't achieve this alone. We need your help, as a community. As students who have always wanted to be "geniuses" but weren't exposed to many opportunities as children, we now want to help the next generation get ahead and achieve their dreams.
