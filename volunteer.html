Why Volunteer

What You Gain From
Volunteering!

Volunteering isn't just about giving back — it's a two-way street. Here is what you gain from volunteering-

☆
Hands-On Experience

Mentor and teach younger students in a fun, low-pressure environment that builds real teaching and leadership skills.

☆
Skill Building

Strengthen communication, leadership, and project management skills that look great on resumes and college applications.

☆
Community Connections

Meet educators, parents, and fellow STEM enthusiasts across San Antonio while building a network you'll carry forward.

☆
Personal Fulfillment

Feel the joy of seeing a student light up when they understand a new concept or finish their first project.

☆
Flexible Commitment

Choose a schedule that fits your life — after school, on weekends, or just for special events.

Your Impact

The Impact Volunteers
Make!

Because our programs are free for families, volunteers directly shape how many students we reach and how impactful each session feels.

☆

Sparking New Passions

Help a student discover a love for science, technology, engineering, or math they might never find elsewhere.

☆

Building Confidence

Guide students through hands-on projects, helping them grow more confident with every experiment.

☆

Real Role Models

Show younger students what's possible by being a relatable, real life example of someone in STEM.

☆

Expanding Our Reach

More volunteers means more sessions, more students served, and higher-quality programs for everyone.

Get Started

How to Volunteer

Getting started is easy - no teaching experience required, just enthusiasm for helping kids explore STEM!

1

Fill Out the Form

Click the volunteer sign-up button below to fill out our volunteer form.

2

Tell Us About You

Share your interests, availability, and any STEM background or skills you'd like to bring.

3

Hear Back From Us

We'll reach out with next steps, including orientation details and upcoming session dates.

4

Attend Orientation

Join a short online orientation to learn about our programs, safety guidelines, and how a typical session runs.

5

Start Volunteering!

Join sessions after school or on selected weekends — no long-term commitment needed to begin.
